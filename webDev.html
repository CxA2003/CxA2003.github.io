The Process

Investigation

During this phase I'm going to understand your brand's objective, your public, and the impression we want to leave

Planning

Together, we'd determine the tools, esthetics and designs that better fit your preferences. My main objective is to help your brand, while the website satisfies you

Design

I'll make a prototype from the information gathered on the previous steps. This will show the order and basic functionality of the website

Construction

After confirming the prototype is to your liking, I'd proceed to make the website you and your brand need

Deployment

After completing the development of your website and it's approval, it'll become visible on the internet, so you and your public could visit it with absolute freedom
